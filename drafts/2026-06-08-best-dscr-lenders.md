## What Is a DSCR Loan and Why Do Investors Use It?

A **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) loan** qualifies based on the rental income a property generates rather than the borrower's personal income. The formula is simple:

> **DSCR = Gross Rental Income ÷ Total Monthly PITIA**
> *(Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance, and any Association dues)*

A property renting for $2,800/month with $2,400 in PITIA has a DSCR of 1.17. Most lenders require 1.0 or higher; stronger ratios (1.25+) unlock better rates. If the property qualifies, you qualify — no W-2s, no tax returns, no debt-to-income calculation.

That matters for three types of investors:

1. **Self-employed investors** whose tax returns show minimal personal income after deductions
2. **Portfolio scalers** who've bumped against conventional lending's 10-property cap
3. **BRRRR investors** who need a permanent rental loan after completing a refinance out of hard money

DSCR loans typically carry 1–2% higher rates than conventional mortgages, require 20–25% down, and often include prepayment penalties (step-down 3-2-1 or 5-4-3-2-1 is standard). They close in an LLC, accept properties you can't finance conventionally (non-warrantable condos, properties with deferred maintenance), and impose no limit on how many properties you finance.

## What Makes a DSCR Lender Good? 5 Criteria That Actually Matter

When evaluating DSCR lenders beyond the rate sheet, these are the factors that separate good lender relationships from headaches:

### 1. Minimum DSCR Threshold
Standard is 1.0. Some lenders accept 0.75–0.90 with compensating factors (lower LTV, higher credit score). A lower DSCR threshold gives you more marginal properties to work with — useful in compressed markets where 1.0 is hard to hit.

### 2. Speed-to-Close
DSCR loans typically close in 21–30 days vs. 10–14 days for hard money. If you're competing with cash buyers, know your lender's actual average — "we can close in 21 days" and "our average is 21 days" are very different statements.

### 3. Prepayment Penalty Structure
A **3-2-1 step-down** penalty charges 3% of the balance if you pay off in year one, 2% in year two, 1% in year three. A **5-4-3-2-1** extends that to five years. If your exit strategy involves refinancing into a conventional loan once rates drop, the shorter penalty saves real money. Model the penalty into your returns before selecting a lender based on rate alone.

### 4. LLC and Entity Flexibility
Most DSCR lenders accept LLC vesting — it's a core feature of the product. Watch for lenders that require personal guarantees even inside an LLC, which partially defeats the asset protection purpose. [VERIFY: Confirm which lenders require personal guarantees on DSCR loans]

### 5. Product Breadth vs. Specialization
A lender like Kiavi or Lima One offers flip, bridge, AND DSCR products. That continuity enables BRRRR in one lending relationship. A specialist like Visio does DSCR exclusively — deeper expertise, lower rates, but you need separate lender relationships for your flip phase.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the minimum DSCR to qualify for a rental loan?

Most lenders require a DSCR of 1.0 or higher — meaning the property's gross rental income equals or exceeds the total PITIA payment. Some lenders (LendingOne, select RCN programs) offer "near-DSCR" products for ratios as low as 0.75, with compensating factors like lower LTV or higher credit score. A DSCR of 1.25+ unlocks the best rates.

### Do DSCR loans require a down payment?

Yes. Most DSCR lenders require 20–25% down (75–80% LTV). A few allow 90% LTV with mortgage insurance, but the rate premium typically erodes the benefit. Budget for 20–25% plus 2–5% in closing costs (origination points, appraisal, title).

### Can I use a DSCR loan to buy my first rental property?

Yes. Lima One Capital, RCN Capital, and Easy Street Capital all accept first-time real estate investors with no prior landlord experience. Expect a slightly lower LTV (70–75%) and higher rates than experienced borrower programs.

### Do DSCR loans work for Airbnb and short-term rentals?

Not all DSCR lenders accept STR income, and those that do vary in how they calculate it. Defy Mortgage and some programs at Lima One and Griffin Funding accept AirDNA data for STR income estimation. Traditional long-term lease comps undervalue STR properties — verify the lender's STR methodology before applying.

### What credit score do I need for a DSCR loan?

Most lenders require a 640–680 minimum. RCN Capital and Defy Mortgage accept as low as 620. Higher scores (700+) materially improve your rate; some lenders price in 50–75 basis point increments by credit tier.

### How long does a DSCR loan take to close?

Expect 21–30 days for most DSCR lenders. Kiavi and Easy Street Capital can close in 14–21 days for well-prepared borrowers. Hard money loans close in 7–14 days but at significantly higher rates — DSCR is the long-term product, hard money is for acquisition speed.
